Rules

Our main rules are:

  • Be a active donator. Try to keep the "Troops Donated" number above the "Troops Recived".
  • If you are new, you must at least say "Hi" before requesting.
  • Follow the range of bases you are given by the war lord.
  • Use both attacks in war except if all bases in your range are 3 starred.
  • Do NOT ask for Elder or Co or there will be an immediate boot.
  • Set your war preference before war starts.

If you fail to follow one of these rules, you will be warned. If asked a second time, you will be demoted or kicked based on your rank.
